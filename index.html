Nahida

Nahida is the current Dendro Archon, also known as Lesser Lord Kusanali.

Keqing

Keqing is a Yuheng of Liyue's Qixing. She is someone who seeks her own answers and chooses her own path with her own power and ability, rather than letting the Gods choose her path.

Furina

Furina is the current vessel of the Hydro Archon and the current ruler of Fontaine. His personality is described by Neuvillete as prone to hysteria, lacking patience and prone to tantrums.

Nilou

A dancer at the Zubayr Theater, Nilou's dancing is elegant and graceful. However, outside of the stage spotlight, she is just like any girl her age; warm, smiling and friendly.

Hu Tao

Hu tao is the 77th director of the Wangsheng Funeral Home, in the Liyue region. She is a mysterious figure who guards the border between life and death. She has a high burden, has a somewhat unusual behavior and loves to make somewhat morbid jokes.
